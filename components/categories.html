Comprehensive Action Taxonomy

The ANUBIS dataset features a carefully designed taxonomy that captures the full spectrum of human actions. Our categorization system distinguishes between independent single-person behaviors and complex multi-person interactions, with special attention to both social and aggressive behaviors.

This diverse collection enables comprehensive evaluation of action recognition models across different complexity levels and interaction types, making ANUBIS a valuable benchmark for advancing the field.
